# Ross Cameron (Warrior Trading) — Trading Philosophy

## Core Philosophy
Momentum day trading on small-cap stocks with catalysts. Trade the strongest stocks
with the highest relative volume in the morning session. Gap-and-go or pullback entries.

## Pre-Market Scanning Criteria (the "gapper" watchlist)
1. **Pre-market gap** — up at least 10% from prior close
2. **Catalyst** — news, earnings, FDA, PR, analyst upgrade (no catalyst = avoid)
3. **Float** — preferably under 10-20M shares (low float = more volatile moves)
4. **Relative volume** — >5x the average volume at that time of day
5. **Price range** — $1–$20 sweet spot (can move 50-100% in one session)

## Gap-and-Go Setup
- Stock gaps up pre-market with catalyst and high relative volume
- At market open, holds above pre-market high
- Enter on first 1-minute or 5-minute candle CLOSE above pre-market high
- Stop: below the open candle low
- Target: measured move (gap size projected) or next round number

## Bull Flag Pullback Setup
- Stock makes a big first move in first 5-10 minutes
- Pulls back in a tight, orderly flag pattern (declining volume on pullback)
- Breaks out of the flag with increasing volume
- Enter on flag breakout candle close
- Stop: below the flag low
- Target: flag pole height projected from breakout

## Filters
- **Time window**: best setups in first 30-90 minutes of market open (9:30–11:00 AM ET)
- **Circuit breakers**: avoid trading immediately after a halt resumes — wait for first 5m candle
- **Market conditions**: works best when overall market is green; avoid on major red days

## Risk Rules (Ross Cameron specific)
- Hard max loss per trade: $100 (or defined % of day's max loss)
- If daily goal hit, consider stopping or going to "scratch mode" (tiny size)
- Size into winners, size out as stock becomes extended

## R:R Minimum
- 2:1 minimum
- Prefer setups where the natural stop is tight (entry close to a clear level)
