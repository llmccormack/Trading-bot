# PB Blake — Trading Philosophy

## Core Philosophy
Price action at key structural levels. Minimal indicators. Let price tell the story.

## Setup Criteria
1. **Higher timeframe bias first** — determine direction on 1h or 4h chart before entering on 5m/15m
2. **Key level identification** — prior day high/low, weekly levels, obvious swing highs/lows
3. **Wait for price to reach the level** — don't anticipate, let price come to you
4. **Confirmation candle** — a rejection candle (pin bar, engulfing, inside bar break) at the level
5. **Volume confirmation** — volume spike on the confirmation candle is a plus

## Entry Rules
- Enter on close of confirmation candle OR on break of confirmation candle's high/low
- Stop behind the level (below support, above resistance) + a small buffer (0.25 ATR)
- Target: next significant structure level

## R:R Minimum
- Only take trades with at least 2:1 R:R
- Prefer 3:1 or better

## What to Avoid
- Entering in the middle of a range
- Chasing price away from a level
- Trading against the higher timeframe trend unless at major structure
- Low volume, choppy sessions

## Market Conditions
- Works best in trending markets with clean structure
- Avoid during news events (wait for initial reaction to settle)
