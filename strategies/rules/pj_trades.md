# PJ Trades — Trading Philosophy

## Core Philosophy
Intraday trend continuation using VWAP as the primary reference. Trade in the direction
price is trending relative to VWAP, entering on pullbacks to VWAP or micro-structure.

## Setup Criteria
1. **VWAP anchor** — price trending above VWAP = bullish bias, below = bearish bias
2. **Market structure** — series of higher highs/higher lows (or lower highs/lower lows)
3. **VWAP pullback** — price pulls back to VWAP and shows rejection
4. **Volume context** — increasing volume on the trend leg, decreasing on pullback
5. **Time filter** — best setups occur in first 2 hours and last hour of session

## Entry Rules
- Long: price above VWAP, pulls back to VWAP, shows bullish rejection candle
- Short: price below VWAP, bounces toward VWAP, shows bearish rejection candle
- Stop: below the rejection candle low (longs) / above the rejection candle high (shorts)
- Target: previous session high/low, or measured move (leg 1 length projected)

## VWAP Deviation Entries
- If price is >1 standard deviation above/below VWAP = potential mean-reversion opportunity
- Enter counter-trend only with clear exhaustion signals (large wick, volume climax)

## Filters
- Don't trade if price is chopping around VWAP — needs a clean directional move first
- Avoid if overall market (ES) is in a range while trading correlated instruments

## R:R Minimum
- 1.5:1 minimum for VWAP plays (tighter stops = still acceptable at lower R:R)
- 2:1 for deviation plays
