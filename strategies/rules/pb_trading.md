# PB Trading — Trading Philosophy

## Core Philosophy
Pullback trading in trending markets. Enter when price retraces to a key moving average
or structure zone, in the direction of the dominant trend.

## Setup Criteria
1. **Trend identification** — EMA 8/21 aligned (8 above 21 for uptrend, 8 below 21 for downtrend)
2. **ADX > 20** — confirms trend has enough strength to trade
3. **Pullback** — price retraces to EMA 21 (or area between 8 and 21 EMAs)
4. **Bounce confirmation** — bullish/bearish rejection candle at the EMA zone
5. **VWAP filter** — price should be above VWAP for longs, below for shorts

## Entry Rules
- Enter on close of the bounce candle
- Stop below the pullback low (for longs) / above pullback high (for shorts)
- Target: prior swing high/low or 2-3x the risk

## Filters
- Avoid pullback entries if price has already made 3+ legs in the same direction without a correction
- Don't enter if the pullback is deeper than EMA 55 (trend may be reversing)
- Skip if ADX is falling sharply — means trend is weakening

## R:R Minimum
- 2:1 minimum, 2.5:1 preferred

## Best Timeframes
- Primary: 5m and 15m for entries
- Bias: 1h and 4h
